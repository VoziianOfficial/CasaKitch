Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how CasaKitch may collect, use, and protect information submitted through the website when homeowners start a kitchen remodeling request.

Platform notice: CasaKitch is not a contractor, installer, retailer, manufacturer, or remodeling company. CasaKitch helps homeowners submit requests and compare local provider options. Any project agreement is made directly between the homeowner and the selected provider.

1. Overview

CasaKitch is an independent kitchen remodeling aggregator and provider-matching platform. The website helps homeowners describe kitchen remodeling goals, submit request details, and compare local provider options.

CasaKitch does not directly perform remodeling work, install cabinets, install countertops, sell materials, manufacture products, act as a retailer, or operate as a remodeling contractor.

2. Information you may provide

When you use a form on the CasaKitch website, you may provide information such as your name, email address, phone number, service interest, project notes, timing preferences, material preferences, kitchen goals, and other request details.

You should only submit information that you are comfortable sharing for the purpose of starting a kitchen remodeling provider comparison request.

3. How information may be used

CasaKitch may use submitted information to review your request, understand your kitchen remodeling goals, respond to your inquiry, organize service categories, and help you compare local provider options.

Submitted information may also be used for website operation, communication, fraud prevention, basic analytics, legal compliance, and improving the clarity of request forms or website content.

4. Provider comparison and request handling

CasaKitch may use your request details to help identify relevant provider paths or local provider options. CasaKitch does not guarantee that any provider will be available, interested, qualified, affordable, or suitable for your project.

Any quote, estimate, inspection, consultation, work agreement, payment, timeline, warranty, or service outcome is handled directly between you and the selected provider.

5. Cookies and website technologies

CasaKitch may use cookies or similar technologies to support website functionality, remember consent choices, improve browsing experience, understand basic usage patterns, and protect forms from misuse.

You can read more in the Cookie Policy.

6. Data sharing

CasaKitch may share request details with relevant provider options, service partners, or operational vendors when needed to respond to your inquiry or support the provider matching process.

CasaKitch does not sell materials, does not directly provide kitchen remodeling work, and does not become a party to any project agreement between you and a selected provider.

7. Security

CasaKitch uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures intended to protect submitted information. However, no website, form, email system, or online transmission method can be guaranteed to be fully secure.

8. Data accuracy

You are responsible for providing accurate request details. Incorrect contact information, unclear project descriptions, or incomplete service details may affect the ability to respond to your inquiry or compare provider options.

9. Third-party links and providers

The CasaKitch website may reference third-party services, providers, or resources. Those third parties operate independently and may have their own privacy practices, terms, policies, pricing, timelines, and service requirements.

10. Your choices

You may choose not to submit a request form. You may also manage cookies through your browser settings or the website cookie banner when available.

To ask questions about submitted information, contact CasaKitch at hello@casakitch.com.
