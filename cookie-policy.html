2. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files that may be stored on your browser or device when you visit a website. Similar technologies may include localStorage, sessionStorage, pixels, tags, scripts, or other tools used to remember choices or understand site behavior.

3. Types of cookies CasaKitch may use

CasaKitch may use essential cookies to make the website work, preference cookies to remember choices such as cookie banner responses, analytics cookies to understand basic usage patterns, and security-related technologies to help protect forms from spam or misuse.

The website should remain usable even if you cancel non-essential cookie use, although some preference or analytics features may be limited.

4. Essential cookies

Essential cookies or local storage items may be used for basic website operation, security, form functionality, navigation, consent storage, and other features needed for the website to function properly.

5. Preference cookies

Preference cookies or local storage may remember choices such as whether you accepted or cancelled the cookie banner. This helps prevent the banner from appearing repeatedly after you make a choice.

6. Analytics cookies

CasaKitch may use analytics cookies or similar tools to understand general website activity, such as page views, device type, browser type, interaction patterns, and performance. These insights may help improve content, layout, forms, and navigation.

Analytics information is generally used in an aggregated or limited way and is not meant to directly perform kitchen remodeling work or make provider guarantees.

7. Third-party technologies

Some website features may rely on third-party libraries, hosting tools, analytics tools, embedded scripts, or other service providers. These third parties may use their own cookies or similar technologies according to their own policies.

CasaKitch does not control all third-party cookie practices. You should review relevant third-party policies if you want more detail about those technologies.

8. Cookie banner choices

The CasaKitch cookie banner may allow you to accept or cancel non-essential cookie use. Your choice may be stored in your browser using localStorage or similar technology.

If you clear your browser data, use another browser, or use another device, you may need to make your cookie choice again.

9. Managing cookies in your browser

Most browsers allow you to delete, block, or manage cookies through browser settings. Blocking cookies may affect website functionality, form behavior, saved preferences, or other features.

10. Provider matching note

Cookies do not change the role of CasaKitch. CasaKitch remains an independent kitchen remodeling aggregator and provider-matching platform. CasaKitch does not directly perform remodeling work, sell materials, install products, or act as a contractor.
